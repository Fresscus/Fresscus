My name is Francesco Vella

My work is mostly based around the moving image and I normally use animation and motion graphics for most of my work. I have always been intrigued by the idea of creating something random and giving it life with the help of animation, which then gives that object a story. I really enjoy creating smooth effects when combining animation with graphic design.

Ever since I started my degree course in digital arts I have been really fascinated by the power of visual effects, the power of creating a new reality in moving images. I am determined to continue exploring and experimenting on this concept in the near future.
